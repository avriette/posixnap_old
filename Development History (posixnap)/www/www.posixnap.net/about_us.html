About us

We, the PosixNap squadron, are a bunch of moderately diverse geeks with a fondness for Unix, hardware, automobiles, money.. and all things good. Rumor has it that the PosixNap network is a very fun place to hang out on for people who don't suck, and you are more than welcome to find out for yourself if that applies to you. We have two servers on the network for your enjoyment: irc.posixnap.net and irc2.posixnap.net, port 6667.
