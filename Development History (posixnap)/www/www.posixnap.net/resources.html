Legend of the Red Dragon

 A few of us are (re)?discovering a MUD called LORD. This is a very entertaining, albeit dated game. It just so happens that we have a few ideas on what we can do to improve on some of the game's downpoints. As a result, Matt Harrington has begun to implement, with some help, his own LORD server in Perl. PLORD's cvs repository is accessible via cvsweb at http://cvsweb.underdogma.net. As it stands, PLORD is a work in progress. If you want to play LORD with us, telnet to alliesplace.com.
